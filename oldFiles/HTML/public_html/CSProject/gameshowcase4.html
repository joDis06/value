Skyrim !

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is an action role-playing videogame developed by Bethesda Game Studios. It is a part of the series "The Elder Scrolls", and is $40, although it does go on sale around the holidays. It was initially released in 2011 and recently received an anniversary update.

Starting a new game in Skyrim sends you into a cutscene. You have been captured and are being sent to be executed. At the end of your wagon ride, you are put onto a line of other captives and are then asked your name. There you are able to design your character, what race you are (Ex: Elves, Reptiles, Cat-like people, etc.), and other physical traits. In the game itself, you follow one main story with the option to do side-quests or just roam the world. You are not forced into quests in a certain order or time-frame, just do as you please and survive.

This is the menu screen, allowing you to create a new save file, load a preexisting one, add community addons, view the credits, or quit.

You start this intro sequence in Helgen, which is destroyed and abandoned (by unforeseen circumstances). Leaving Helgen sends you into a small, tutorial like portion, where you learn the basics of combat and movement. After, you end up outside of the mountain and must continue on to the other towns to progress.

In the game, pressing the [Tab] key will bring you to a small menu. Opening the "Skills" section brings you here. "Skills" are different aspects to your character that level up as you play. In addition, leveling up your overall character as seen on the top (Level 15 with an 'experience' bar beside it), will do 2 things: Give you the option to increase Magicka, Health, or Stamina by 10, and give you 1 perk point to get better skills. How you decide will build the framework of your playstyle as time goes on.
