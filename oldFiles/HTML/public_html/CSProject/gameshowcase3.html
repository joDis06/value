Stardew Valley !

Stardew Valley is a role-playing simulation game developed by ConcernedApe. It was initially released in 2016, costs $15, and is available on all platforms.

Stardew Valley is a farming, semi-roleplaying game in which you play as the grandson of a man, living out his dream of being on a farm in a small village. As you complete quests, level up skills, and obtain more equipment, the story is further revealed. Will you better the town or will you join the evil company that had its grasp on your grandfather long ago?

This is the menu screen, allowing you to create a new save file, load a preexisting one, play together with others, or exit. Game settings are more available once the game has started.

After a short cutscene you are shown waking up in your house. The house includes some basic furniture and a gift of 'Parsnip seeds'. You will receive a quest to plant and harvest 5 parsnips and too converse with the other folks in the town. From this point on you are free to do what you will with your farm.

As a note, Stardew Valley includes seasons, each lasting 28 days. Each season has its own collectibles, fish, and crops that are only available within those 4 weeks. Once the season has ended, they cannot be obtained until the season returns. You have no time limit on how many years pass to get items (every 4 seasons a year passes), however, so do not worry. Otherwise, have fun farming!
