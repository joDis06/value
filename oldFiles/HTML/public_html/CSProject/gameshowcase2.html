Starbound !

Starbound is another action-adventure video game developed by Chucklefish, initally releasted in 2016. It costs $15 and is available on computers and consoles.

Starbound is similar to Terraria but with a more structured story / quest-line. After creating your character, you are thrown into a ship. In said ship is an assembly of characters discussing the invention of a new tool. Unfortunately, it is interrupted by a giant monster, calling for an evacuation. You wind up on a ship and have to fend for yourself.

This is the menu screen, the first screen you see as the game is launched. It is a little more limited than Terraria's, only allowing for settings and beginning the game.

In the game you are first given a broken sword. This screenshot, however is from later in the game. Opening your inventory with [I] allows you to see your stats, items, and equipment. From there you can decide what kind of items you want to use, throw out, sell, and much more as you interact with your environment.
