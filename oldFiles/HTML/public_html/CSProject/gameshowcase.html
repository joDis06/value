Terraria !

Terraria is an open world, action-adventure game developed by Re-Logic, initally releasted in 2011. It costs $10 and is available on computer, mobile, and consoles.

In Terraria you break down trees, talk to characters, mine underground, and defeat enemies as you build whatever comes to your imagination. With a variety of difficulties, world settings, and other customization within the game itself, nobody feels left unsatisfied.

This is the menu screen, the first screen you see as the game is launched. You may adjust your settings, look through community made addons, or begin the game.

Once you enter your first world, these items are given to you: A copper shortsword, a copper pickaxe, and a copper axe. With these 3 items you are able to gather basic materials and start your journey.
